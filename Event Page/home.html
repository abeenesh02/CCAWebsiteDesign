Connecting you
to endless opportunities

The Canadian Council on Africa (CCAfrica) is the only pan-Canadian and national organization dedicated to
promoting Canadian technologies, know-how, and expertise. With offices from coast to coast, our network
includes key decision makers from every sector of the economy representing both the private and public sector. As
a not-for-profit organization, our core mission is to promote trade and economic development between Canada
and the continent of Africa.
