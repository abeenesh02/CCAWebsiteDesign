Trade and Investment
Opportunities
Special events, conferences and meetings are a core function of CCAfrica and one of the key services provided to members. Our event programs provide CCAfrica members with exclusive networking opportunities, educational programming and access to senior government officials.

Interested in sponsoring an upcoming event? Supporting CCAfricas events through corporate sponsorship is a great way to maximize your brand exposure in the market, show your support for trade and investment in Africa and help make these events possible through underwriting costs. For more information on CCAfricas sponsorship opportunities, contact the Director of Special Events.
