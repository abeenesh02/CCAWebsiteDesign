Trade Missions
Trade and Investment
Opportunities
The Canadian Council on Africa’s trade missions are programs on the continent designed to help companies explore trade and investment opportunities in African markets. Our trade missions serve as excellent door-openers that help Canada firms establish strategic connections with local business communities in Africa, and facilitate access to key economic decision-makers and senior government officials. Our trade missions offer participants the opportunity to:

-  Meet and network with senior government officials (group and private

meetings)

-  Explore business opportunities in Africa's most promising sectors

-  Gain a deeper understanding of investment climates

-  Discover new business partners during meetings with local business leaders

-  Obtain real-time leads that can result in sales and contracts
